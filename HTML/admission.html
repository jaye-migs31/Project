About Admission

The Admissions Office of SVFC is the welcoming gateway for students and families seeking quality Catholic education. We are committed to guiding applicants through a smooth and transparent admissions process, beginning with online application and document submission, followed by entrance testing and evaluation. Our team ensures each applicant is fairly assessed based on academic performance, conduct, and alignment with Vincentian values. We support families at every stage, from inquiry to enrollment, promoting a nurturing and faith-driven environment where students grow intellectually, morally, and spiritually. Your journey with SVFC starts here.
